    High Striker

 Prev project Next project 

The objective of this game is to light up the most LED’s by hitting the pressure sensor at the bottom. To begin making this game, an Arduino microprocessor was programmed to light a certain amount of LED’s based on the value of force that the sensor is receiving as the player hits the sensor. This sensor works as a resistance and its value changes depending on the pressure it receives.

Materials for High Striker


  • 6.5" x 2.1" Solderless Breadboard
  • 10k Ohm Resistor
  • Square FSR
  • Jumper Wires Pack
  • Arduino Uno
  • USB 2.0 A-B Cable (Arduino Cable)
  • LED's (red, yellow, and green)

The materials shown below are the electronic components needed. For construction purposes, recycled materials are used. For more information in the list of materials, download the Students Manual provided below. The Manual provides every detail needed for the construction of the High Striker.

A Manual is also provided for teachers and educators containing every detail needed for mentoring students for the development of the High Striker with basic engineering concepts.
